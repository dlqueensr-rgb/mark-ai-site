How it works

Two jobs, one system: answer the phone, and work the phone.

Most AI tools just pick up calls. Mark runs both directions — inbound and outbound — off the same brain that already knows your pricing, your schedule, and your trade.

Inbound

Never miss another call

Every ring is answered, triaged, and turned into a booked job on the calendar.

  • 01Answers instantly — every call, every hour, no voicemail, no hold music.
  • 02Triages the job — asks the right questions for the trade and the problem.
  • 03Quotes or estimates — gives a price, or books an onsite estimate when one's needed.

  • 04Books the result — every interested call lands back on your schedule.
