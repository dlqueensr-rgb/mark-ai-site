Campaign types

Every stage of the customer relationship — automated.

Five campaign types cover the full lifecycle: from a customer's first job to their sixth. Each type runs independently, targets the right segment, and fires without anyone touching a button.

Fall, winter, spring, and summer campaigns reach every dialable customer with a tiered labor savings offer. Campaigns activate on the first day of each season and roll at your chosen daily rate until everyone's reached.

How it works

Set it up in an afternoon. Run it for years.

1

Set your offer and pace

Choose your discount amount, expiry window, and how many customers to contact per day. Flat dollar, percentage, or tiered by job size — the offer editor handles all of it. Blast your whole base at once for holiday promos, or drip at your own pace for seasonal campaigns.

2

Mark handles everything

Campaigns enroll new customers automatically every morning. SMS goes first, email 24 hours later — both with your tracking number and your offer. Holiday blasts fire when the window opens and stop when it closes. Seasonal campaigns activate on the right month without you touching anything.

3

Watch results in your dashboard

Every campaign shows live enrollment counts, SMS and email sent, opens, and bookings attributed back to the campaign. When you want to reach your base again, hit Re-enroll and the entire cycle runs again — fresh pool, same campaign, zero setup.

What your customers receive

Branded emails that look like you spent all day on them.

Every campaign sends a professionally designed email with your logo, your tracking number, and your offer — rendered automatically for each customer. No templates to manage, no designer needed.

  • ✓Your branding, automatically applied — logo, colors, and tracking number on every send.
  • ✓Offer pulled from your dashboard — change the discount once, every future email reflects it.
  • ✓Open tracking built in — see who opened, campaign by campaign, in your dashboard.
  • ✓SMS goes first, email follows — customers get a text within your send window, email 24 hours later.
