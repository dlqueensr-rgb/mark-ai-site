Your privacy choices

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

California residents have the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. Here's where we stand and how to make a request.

Last updated: June 28, 2026

We do not sell your personal information

Mark AI does not sell personal information for money, and we do not "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (collectively, "CCPA").

When we provide our office-manager service to a business, we process information on that business's behalf as a service provider, using it only to provide the Service — not to sell or share it for our own purposes.

Even though we do not sell or share personal information, we provide this page and an opt-out mechanism so your choice is always easy to record. If our practices ever change, your opt-out on file will be honored.

Your right to opt out

If you are a California resident, you have the right to direct a business not to sell or share your personal information. To submit a do-not-sell-or-share request to Mark AI, email support@markaioffice.com with the subject line "Do Not Sell or Share" and include enough information for us to identify you. We will record your request and confirm it.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

Where required by law, we honor opt-out preference signals such as the Global Privacy Control (GPC) sent by your browser or device. If you enable GPC, we will treat it as a valid request to opt out of any sale or sharing for the browser or device from which the signal is sent.

Authorized agents

You may use an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. We may ask the agent for proof that you gave them permission, and we may ask you to verify your own identity directly.

Requests about a business's data

If your information was provided to Mark AI by a business that uses our Service (for example, because you are that business's customer), the business controls that data. Please direct your request to that business; we will support them in responding as required.

No discrimination

We will not deny you services, charge you a different price, or provide you a different level of quality because you exercised your privacy rights.

More information

For full details on how we handle personal information, see our Privacy Policy. Questions can be sent to support@markaioffice.com.
